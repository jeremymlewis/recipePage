Cherry Crunch Pie

Every Thanksgiving, families around the country eat pie. My grandma would always make the most delicous pie! This is a very easy recipe for a tasty but simple cherry pie that we like to call "Cherry Crunch Pie"

    Ingredients

  • 1 can cherry pie filling
  • 1/2 pkg. white or yellow cake mix
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts
  • 1/2 cup melted butter

Put pie filling in 8 x 8 pan. Sprinkle dry cake mix over filling. Add nuts, pour butter over top. Bake at 375 for 45 minutes. Top with ship cream or ice cream.
