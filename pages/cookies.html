Cookies

I made these cookies on a date with a girl, and now we are engaged! They are very tasty and I love them. They bring back good memeories, and are an old family recipe.

    Ingredients

  • 1 cup shortening (part butter)
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 3/4 cups flour
  • 2 tsp. cream of tartar
  • 1 tsp. soda
  • 1/4 tsp. salt

Mix shortening, sugar and eggs. Mix flour, cream of tartar, soda and salt; stir in. Form into balls the size of walnuts. Roll in 2 Tbs. sugar and 2 Tbs. cinnamon. Place 2" apart on ungreased sheet. Bake at 400 for 8 minutes.
