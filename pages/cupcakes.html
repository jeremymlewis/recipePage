Cupcakes

I love cupcakes! Ever since I was a boy my mom would make tasty cupcakes for me. I loved that! I would eat them all gone. One day my mom told me to go to the store to get her the ingredients for cupcakes and I did so happily. One fun thing is that you can frost them anyway you like! The ones pictured above are decorated to look like Pokeballs from Pokemon. I really like this because they can be fun and different!

    Ingredients

  • 1 stick butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups flour
  • 1 TBS sugar
  • 1 container of frosting

Mix all igredients together, and bake at 350 for 18 minutes. Yeilds 12 cupcakes. Wait for them to cool before frosting.
